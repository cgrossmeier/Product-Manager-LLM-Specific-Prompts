# Storytelling: Customer Adoption Arc
**Platform:** ChatGPT (GPT-4 / GPT-5)

## System Message
You are a narrative strategist who crafts compelling customer success stories that demonstrate product value through authentic transformation arcs.

## User Message
Create a customer adoption story.

**Customer:** [Company/industry]
**Challenge:** [Problem faced]
**Audience:** [Who reads this]
**Format:** [Case study/video/presentation]

### Story Structure:

**Act 1 - The Challenge:**
- Initial situation and stakes
- Failed attempts and frustration
- Specific pain points
- Decision to change

**Act 2 - The Journey:**
- Discovery and evaluation
- Implementation experience
- Early wins and momentum
- Challenges overcome

**Act 3 - The Transformation:**
- Quantified results
- Unexpected benefits
- Cultural/operational change
- Future vision

### Story Requirements:

**Elements:**
- Hero: Customer (not product)
- Mentor: Product as enabler
- Villain: Status quo/problem
- Transformation: Measurable change

**Details:**
- 3-5 direct customer quotes
- 4-6 specific metrics
- Concrete examples
- Emotional moments

**Format:** 750-1000 word narrative with:
- Compelling title
- Executive summary
- Three-act structure
- Metrics sidebar
- Call to action

Make it authentic, specific, emotional, credible, and inspiring.

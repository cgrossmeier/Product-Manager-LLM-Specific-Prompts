# 25 Enhancements to Improve Customer Adoption
**Platform:** ChatGPT (GPT-4 / GPT-5)

## System Message
You are a growth product manager specializing in user adoption and activation. You identify high-impact enhancements that remove friction and accelerate customer success.

## User Message
Generate 25 enhancement ideas to improve customer adoption.

**Product:** [Name/description]
**Current State:** [Adoption metrics, conversion funnel data]
**Target:** [Desired improvement]

### Requirements:
Generate 25 distinct ideas with this format:

| # | Category | Enhancement | Impact | Effort | Priority | Rationale |
|---|----------|-------------|--------|--------|----------|-----------|

**Categories:**
- Onboarding (first-time experience)
- Education (learning and understanding)
- Friction reduction
- Quick wins (fast value demonstration)
- Integration & workflow fit
- Social proof & trust

**Impact:** High/Medium/Low improvement to adoption
**Effort:** Weeks/Months to implement
**Priority:** 1-5 (5=critical)

**Analysis:**
- Map to adoption funnel stages
- Identify quick wins
- Recommend top 10 for roadmap

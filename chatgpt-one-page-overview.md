# One-Page Product Overview for Executive Team
**Platform:** ChatGPT (GPT-4 / GPT-5)

## System Message
You are an executive communications specialist who creates compelling one-page overviews that distill complex products into digestible executive summaries. Your documents are visually scannable, data-driven, and action-oriented.

## User Message
Create a one-page product overview for executive audiences.

**Product:** [Name]
**Market:** [Target segment]
**Audience:** [CEO/Board/Investors]

### Required Sections (all must fit on one page):
1. Header: Name, tagline, vision (3 lines max)
2. Opportunity: Market problem and size (2-3 sentences)
3. Solution: What we do, how we're different (2 paragraphs)
4. Business Model & Traction: Metrics table
5. Roadmap: Now/Next/Future (3 bullets each)
6. Competitive Advantage: 3 key differentiators
7. Team: Leadership and strengths (2-3 sentences)
8. Financials: Key numbers (if applicable)
9. The Ask: What's needed from audience

**Format:** Executive-friendly with visual hierarchy, charts described, emphasizing business outcomes.

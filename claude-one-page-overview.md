# One-Page Product Overview for Executive Team

**Platform:** Claude (Sonnet 4.5 / Opus 4)

---

## Prompt

I need your help creating a compelling one-page product overview designed for executive audiences (CEO, Board, investors, senior leadership). This document should communicate our product strategy, value proposition, and business impact in a format busy executives can absorb in 2-3 minutes.

**Product Context:**
- Product name: [Your product]
- Target market: [Industry/segment]
- Stage: [Early/Growth/Mature]
- Audience: [Who will read this - Board, investors, exec team, etc.]

## One-Page Structure

### Header Section

**Product Name & Tagline**
[Compelling one-liner that captures essence]

**Vision Statement**
[One sentence describing the future we're creating]

**Mission**
[What we're building and for whom]

### The Opportunity (1-2 paragraphs max)

**Market Problem:**
What critical problem exists that creates this opportunity? Quantify the pain.

**Market Size:**
- TAM: $X billion
- Our target: $Y million (Year 3)

**Why Now:**
What market dynamics or trends make this the right time?

### Our Solution (2-3 short paragraphs)

**What We Do:**
Describe the product and core capabilities in plain language

**How We're Different:**
2-3 unique differentiators that create competitive moat

**Who It's For:**
Primary customer segments and use cases

### Business Model & Traction

**How We Make Money:**
Pricing model in one sentence

**Key Metrics** (as applicable):
- Customers: [X]
- ARR/Revenue: [$Y]
- Growth rate: [Z%]
- NRR: [%]
- Customer satisfaction: [NPS or CSAT]

### Product Roadmap (High-Level)

**Now:** [Current capabilities]

**Next (3-6 months):** [Top 2-3 priorities]

**Future (6-12 months):** [Strategic direction]

### Competitive Advantage

Why we'll win:
- [Advantage 1]
- [Advantage 2]
- [Advantage 3]

### Team & Execution

**Leadership:** [Key leaders and relevant background]

**Team Size:** [Headcount]

**Key Strengths:** [What makes this team uniquely qualified]

### Financial Overview (if appropriate)

**Funding:** [$X raised, Series Y]

**Burn:** [$X/month]

**Runway:** [X months]

**Path to Profitability:** [Brief statement]

### The Ask (if relevant)

What decision or support is needed from this audience?

---

## Output Requirements

**Format Guidelines:**
- Must fit on ONE page when formatted
- Use visual hierarchy (headers, bold, bullets)
- Include 1-2 key charts/graphics (describe what to include)
- Executive-friendly language (no jargon)
- Emphasize business outcomes over features
- Every word must earn its place

**Visual Elements to Include:**
- Market size visualization
- Growth trajectory chart
- Customer logos or case study highlight
- Product screenshot or conceptual diagram
- Competitive positioning map

**Tone:**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Data-driven
- Forward-looking
- Action-oriented

---

Please create a one-page overview that can be quickly scanned but also rewards deeper reading, with the most critical information emphasized visually and positioned in the top half of the page.

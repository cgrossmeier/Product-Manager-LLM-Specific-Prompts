# Storytelling: Customer Adoption Arc
**Platform:** Claude (Sonnet 4.5 / Opus 4)

## Prompt
Help me craft a compelling customer adoption story that demonstrates our product's impact.

**Customer Profile:**
- Company: [Name/industry/size]
- Challenge: [Problem they faced]
- Role: [Decision maker and users]

**Story Purpose:**
- Audience: [Prospects, investors, team, etc.]
- Goal: [What we want audience to take away]
- Format: [Case study, video script, presentation, etc.]

## Story Arc Structure

### Act 1: The Challenge (Before)
**Setup the World:**
- What was the customer's situation?
- What problem were they struggling with?
- What was at stake if unsolved?
- What had they tried that didn't work?

**Make it Relatable:**
- Specific details that bring it to life
- Emotional impact of the problem
- Business metrics showing the pain

### Act 2: The Journey (During)
**Discovery:**
- How did they find us?
- What made them take a chance?
- Initial skepticism or concerns?

**Implementation:**
- What was the onboarding/setup experience?
- Key moments or surprises
- Challenges encountered and overcome
- Internal champion's role

**First Wins:**
- Quick wins or proof points
- Moment they knew this would work
- Building momentum

### Act 3: The Transformation (After)
**Results:**
- Quantified business impact
- Before/after comparison
- Unexpected benefits

**Expansion:**
- How usage grew
- Additional use cases discovered
- Became embedded in workflows

**Advocacy:**
- Customer becoming champion
- Sharing with peers
- Future vision

## Story Elements

**Hero:** [The customer/user, not our product]
**Mentor:** [Our product as guide/enabler]
**Villain:** [The problem/status quo]
**Transformation:** [Specific change achieved]

**Compelling Details:**
- Direct quotes from customer
- Specific metrics (3-4 key numbers)
- Emotional moments
- Surprising insights
- Concrete examples

## Output Format

Create a narrative story (750-1000 words) structured as:

**Title:** [Compelling headline]

**Summary:** [2-3 sentence overview]

**The Challenge:** [3-4 paragraphs setting up problem]

**The Solution:** [2-3 paragraphs on implementation]

**The Results:** [2-3 paragraphs on outcomes]

**Looking Forward:** [1 paragraph on future]

**Sidebar:** Key metrics and quotes

Make it:
- Authentic (real customer voice)
- Specific (concrete details)
- Emotional (human impact)
- Credible (honest about challenges)
- Inspiring (transformation achieved)

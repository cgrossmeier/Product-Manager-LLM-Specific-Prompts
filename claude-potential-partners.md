# Potential Partners for Product or Service
**Platform:** Claude (Sonnet 4.5 / Opus 4)

## Prompt
Help me identify and evaluate potential strategic partners for our product.

**Product:** [Name and description]
**Partnership Goals:** [What we're trying to achieve]
**Stage:** [Our company/product maturity]

## Partnership Strategy Framework

### Partnership Types to Consider:

**Technology Integrations:**
- Complementary products to integrate with
- Platform partnerships (AWS, Azure, Salesforce, etc.)
- API and data partnerships

**Go-to-Market:**
- Reseller or distribution partners
- Referral partnerships
- Co-marketing partnerships

**Strategic:**
- Ecosystem plays
- Industry alliances
- Standards bodies or consortiums

### Partner Evaluation Criteria

For each potential partner, assess:

**Strategic Fit:**
- Audience overlap: [1-10 score]
- Value proposition alignment: [1-10]
- Mutual benefit: [1-10]
- Strategic importance: [High/Med/Low]

**Opportunity Size:**
- Addressable customers: [Estimate]
- Revenue potential: [$X annually]
- Market access value: [What markets this opens]

**Execution Feasibility:**
- Technical integration effort: [Weeks/months]
- Sales enablement needed: [Effort level]
- Relationship strength: [Existing/warm/cold]
- Decision timeframe: [How long to close]

**Risks:**
- Competitive conflicts
- Dependency risks
- Brand alignment issues

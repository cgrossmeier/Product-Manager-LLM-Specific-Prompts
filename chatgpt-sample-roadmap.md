# Sample Roadmap with Dependencies and Timeline
**Platform:** ChatGPT (GPT-4 / GPT-5)

## System Message
You are a product strategist who creates clear, strategic roadmaps that balance ambition with realism and communicate effectively to diverse audiences.

## User Message
Create a product roadmap showing direction, dependencies, and timeline.

**Product:** [Name]
**Timeframe:** [6/12/18 months]
**Audience:** [Stakeholders]
**Strategic Goals:** [Objectives]

### Roadmap Structure:

**Now/Next/Later Format:**

| Timeframe | Theme | Initiatives | Value | Dependencies | Confidence |
|-----------|-------|-------------|-------|--------------|------------|
| Now (Q1) | [Theme] | [3-5 items] | [Impact] | [Blockers] | High |
| Next (Q2-Q3) | [Theme] | [3-5 items] | [Impact] | [Needs] | Medium |
| Later (Q4+) | [Theme] | [Direction] | [Vision] | [TBD] | Low |

**For Each Initiative:**
- Customer value proposition
- Success metrics
- Timeline (start, beta, GA)
- Dependencies (technical, product, external)
- Resource requirements
- Risk level

**Dependency Map:**
Describe critical path and parallel workstreams

**Audience-Specific Views:**
- Executive: Themes and business impact
- Customer: Value and rough timing
- Internal: Detailed specs and dates

**Elements:**
- Vision statement
- Strategic themes
- Quarterly milestones
- Resource allocation
- Risk assessment
- Success metrics

Make it outcome-focused, realistic, flexible, and strategically aligned.

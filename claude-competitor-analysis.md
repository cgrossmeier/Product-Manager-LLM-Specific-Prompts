# Market Research: Competitor Analysis

**Platform:** Claude (Sonnet 4.5 / Opus 4)

---

## Prompt

I need your help conducting a comprehensive competitor analysis for my product. Act as an expert competitive intelligence analyst who can assess market dynamics, identify competitive threats and opportunities, and develop strategic positioning recommendations.

**Product Context:**
- Our product: [Name and description]
- Target market: [Segment, industry, geography]
- Stage: [Early / Growth / Mature]
- Unique value proposition: [What makes you different]

**Competitors to Analyze:**
- Direct competitors: [List 3-5 main competitors]
- Indirect competitors: [Alternative solutions]
- Emerging threats: [New entrants or pivoting companies]

**Available Intelligence:**
- Competitor websites/marketing materials
- Customer reviews (G2, Capterra, etc.)
- Public financial data or funding info
- Product documentation or trials
- Sales competitive intel
- Industry analyst reports

## Analysis Framework

### For Each Major Competitor:

**Company Overview**
- Founded, funding, size, leadership
- Mission and strategic direction
- Market position and brand perception
- Recent news or significant moves

**Product Capabilities**

Analyze across these dimensions:

*Core Functionality*
- Key features and capabilities
- Technical architecture approach
- AI/ML implementation (if applicable)
- Integration ecosystem
- Platform vs. point solution

*User Experience*
- Interface quality and design
- Onboarding and time-to-value
- Learning curve
- Mobile/cross-platform support

*Performance & Reliability*
- Speed and responsiveness
- Uptime and reliability
- Scalability characteristics
- Known technical limitations

*AI-Specific Analysis (if relevant)*
- Model types and approaches
- Data requirements
- Accuracy/performance benchmarks
- Explainability and transparency
- Responsible AI practices

**Go-to-Market Strategy**

*Pricing & Packaging*
- Pricing model (per-user, usage-based, tiered, etc.)
- Price points at each tier
- Free tier or trial offering
- Enterprise vs. SMB approaches
- Value-for-money perception

*Target Customers*
- Ideal customer profile
- Primary segments served
- Geographic focus
- Company size sweet spot

*Sales & Distribution*
- Sales model (PLG, sales-led, hybrid)
- Channel strategy
- Partner ecosystem
- Marketing positioning
- Customer acquisition tactics

**Customer Perception**

Based on reviews, case studies, and public feedback:

*Strengths*
- What customers consistently praise
- Standout features or capabilities
- Service/support quality
- Use cases where they excel

*Weaknesses*
- Common complaints
- Feature gaps
- Support/service issues
- Use cases where they struggle

*Net Promoter Score / Sentiment*
- Overall customer satisfaction level
- Would-recommend rate
- Churn indicators

**Strategic Positioning**

*Market Position*
- Category leadership vs. challenger
- Market share estimates
- Growth trajectory
- Strategic focus areas

*Differentiation*
- Primary competitive advantages
- Unique capabilities or IP
- Brand strengths
- Market category definition

*Vulnerabilities*
- Weaknesses we could exploit
- Segments they ignore
- Technology debt or limitations
- Strategic misalignments

## Competitive Landscape Synthesis

After analyzing individual competitors, provide:

### Market Map
Visualize (describe) competitors on key axes:
- X-axis: [e.g., Ease of Use → Power/Complexity]
- Y-axis: [e.g., SMB Focus → Enterprise Focus]

Show where each competitor sits and where white space exists.

### Competitive Dynamics

**Market Leaders**
- Who sets category standards?
- Dominant players and their moats
- Barriers to entry they've created

**Fast Followers**
- Well-funded challengers gaining share
- Differentiation strategies
- Growth rates

**Niche Players**
- Specialists serving specific segments
- Unique positioning or technology
- Potential disruptors

## Strategic Recommendations

Provide actionable insights on:

**Differentiation Strategy**
- Where can we be meaningfully different and better?
- What capabilities should we build/emphasize?
- What markets/segments should we own?

**Competitive Positioning**
- How should we position against each competitor?
- What messaging resonates given their weaknesses?
- Which battles should we fight vs. avoid?

**Win-Loss Scenarios**
- When do we win against each competitor?
- When do we lose, and why?
- How to improve win rates?

**Strategic Moves**
- Offensive opportunities (where to attack)
- Defensive priorities (where to protect)
- Market gaps to capture
- Partnerships to pursue

**Monitoring Plan**
- What signals to track for each competitor?
- Early warning indicators
- Quarterly review cadence

## Output Format

Please structure as:
1. Executive Summary (key findings and recommendations)
2. Individual Competitor Deep Dives
3. Competitive Landscape Analysis
4. Feature Comparison Matrix
5. Strategic Positioning Recommendations
6. Action Plan and Monitoring Framework

---

## Tips for Best Results

- Distinguish between what competitors say vs. what they actually deliver
- Look for trends in recent product updates and announcements
- Consider not just current state but trajectory and momentum
- Identify early signals of strategic shifts
- Note areas where the market is still evolving or unsettled

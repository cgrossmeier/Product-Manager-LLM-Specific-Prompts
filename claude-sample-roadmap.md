# Sample Roadmap with Dependencies and Timeline
**Platform:** Claude (Sonnet 4.5 / Opus 4)

## Prompt
Create a product roadmap that shows our strategic direction with clear dependencies and realistic timelines.

**Product:** [Name]
**Horizon:** [6, 12, or 18 months]
**Audience:** [Internal team, exec team, customers, board]
**Goals:** [Strategic objectives this roadmap supports]

## Roadmap Framework

### Now/Next/Later Structure

**Now (Current Quarter):**
Features in development or releasing soon

**Next (Next 1-2 Quarters):**
Committed work with solid scope

**Later (Future Quarters):**
Strategic direction, less detailed

### For Each Major Initiative:

**Initiative Name:** [Clear, customer-facing name]

**Description:** [1-2 sentences of value, not features]

**Customer Value:** [Problems solved, benefits delivered]

**Success Metrics:** [How we'll measure success]

**Timeline:**
- Start: [Quarter/month]
- Beta: [Quarter/month]
- GA: [Quarter/month]

**Dependencies:**
- Technical: [Platform/infrastructure needed first]
- Product: [Other features that must complete]
- External: [Partnerships, integrations, etc.]
- Resources: [Teams or skills required]

**Confidence Level:**
- High (committed, well-scoped)
- Medium (probable, some unknowns)
- Low (exploratory, may change)

**Strategic Theme:**
[Which company objective this advances]

### Dependency Visualization

Describe a dependency map showing:
- Critical path items
- Parallel workstreams
- Blocking relationships
- Resource constraints

### Risk Callouts

For each quarter, note:
- Capacity risks (team availability)
- Technical risks (unknown complexity)
- External dependencies (partner readiness)
- Market timing risks

## Roadmap Formats

**For Each Audience, Create:**

**Executive/Board Version:**
- High-level themes and outcomes
- Business impact focus
- Strategic rationale
- Resource requirements
- Risk summary

**Customer-Facing Version:**
- Value-oriented language
- No internal dates
- Disclaimer about changes
- Feedback mechanism

**Internal Team Version:**
- Detailed features and specs
- Technical dependencies
- Resource allocation
- Sprint-level timing
- Success criteria

## Output Structure

**Vision:** [Where we're headed long-term]

**Strategic Themes:** [3-5 focus areas]

**Quarterly Breakdown:**

| Quarter | Theme | Key Initiatives | Dependencies | Confidence |
|---------|-------|----------------|--------------|------------|
| Q1 2025 | [Theme] | [3-5 items] | [Blockers] | High/Med/Low |

**Critical Path:** [Major milestones and dependencies]

**Resource Plan:** [Team allocation by quarter]

**Success Metrics:** [How we track progress]

**Assumptions:** [What must remain true]

Make the roadmap:
- Outcome-focused (value, not features)
- Realistic (achievable timelines)
- Flexible (capacity for learning)
- Transparent (honest about risks)
- Strategic (aligned with company goals)

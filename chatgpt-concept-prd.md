### PRD Requirements

#### Section 1: Executive Summary (Critical)

Craft a compelling one-paragraph summary containing:
1. **Opportunity:** Market opportunity in one sentence
2. **Problem:** Customer pain point with severity
3. **Solution:** Our approach in simple terms
4. **Impact:** Expected business outcomes (revenue, growth, strategic)
5. **Investment:** Total cost and timeline
6. **Why Now:** Timing rationale

**Format this as:** A single, dense paragraph that executives can read in 30 seconds and understand the complete picture.

### Formatting Guidelines

**Executive-Friendly Formatting:**
- Use tables for data-heavy sections
- Bold key numbers and metrics
- Use bullet points over dense paragraphs
- Include visual descriptions (what charts to create)
- Keep sections modular (executives can jump to relevant parts)
- Use color coding: Green (on-track), Yellow (risk), Red (blocker)

**Tone:**
- Confident but not overconfident
- Data-driven but acknowledges uncertainty
- Honest about risks while showing mitigation
- Action-oriented and decisive

**Length:**
- Aim for 6-8 pages when formatted
- Executive summary must fit on one page
- Appendix for supporting details

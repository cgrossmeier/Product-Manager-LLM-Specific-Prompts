# 25 Enhancements to Reduce tickets
**Platform:** Claude (Sonnet 4.5 / Opus 4)

## Prompt
Suggest 25 significant enhancements that would reduce tickets.

## User Message
Generate 25 enhancement ideas to reduce tickets.

Step 1. Ask for the following details.  Manually enter data or upload a document.
**Product:** [Description]
**Current Metrics:** [Baseline]
**Target:** [Goal]
**Support Ticket History:** [topics with support count]

Step 2. Analyze Data
Identify friction points in product, process, and user experience that generate the highest volume of support tickets.  

**Analysis:** 
Prioritize the top 10 and provide an implementation roadmap.  

**Output**
Provide a Product Enhancement PRD that addresses the top customer support and friction points.  Provide a summary of the likely impact these issues are having on usability, scalability, revenue, and product adoption.

Generate 25 ideas in this format:

| # | Enhancement | Impact | Effort | Priority | Rationale |
|---|-------------|--------|--------|----------|-----------|

After listing all 25:
- **Quick Wins:** Easiest to implement with good ROI
- **Strategic Priorities:** Highest impact on goal
- **Roadmap Recommendation:** Sequencing for maximum effect

## Language to Avoid

**Prohibited punctuation:** em dash, en dash, or extended hyphens (use commas, semicolons, or periods instead)

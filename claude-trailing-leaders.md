## Prompt
Conduct an honest assessment of where our product trails market leaders and what we should do about it.

**Our Product:** [Name]
**Market Leaders:** [List top 3-5]

## Analysis Framework

### Capability Gaps
For each area where leaders excel and we don't:

**Gap:** [Specific capability or feature]
**Leader Benchmark:** [What best-in-class looks like]
**Our Current State:** [Where we are]
**Impact:** [How much this matters to customers]
**Difficulty to Close:** [How hard to catch up]
**Strategic Priority:** [Should we close this gap?]

### Categories:
- Core product capabilities
- User experience & design
- Performance & reliability
- Integration ecosystem
- Support & service
- Brand & market presence
- Pricing & packaging
- Sales & distribution

### Strategic Response Options

For each gap, recommend:
1. **Close Aggressively:** Critical to compete, invest heavily
2. **Close Selectively:** Important but differentiate approach
3. **Accept Gap:** Not our battle to fight, focus elsewhere
4. **Leapfrog:** Jump ahead with next-gen solution

**Recommendations:**
- Top 5 gaps to close immediately
- Top 3 areas to leapfrog
- Gaps to accept and work around
- Timeline and investment required

### Strategic Synthesis

#### SWOT Cross-Analysis

**SO Strategies (Strength + Opportunity):**
How can we use our strengths to capitalize on opportunities?

Example: "Leverage our superior AI accuracy (strength) to win enterprise customers adopting AI for first time (opportunity)"

**WO Strategies (Weakness + Opportunity):**
How can we overcome weaknesses to pursue opportunities?

Example: "Partner to fill product gaps (weakness mitigation) to enter adjacent market (opportunity)"

**ST Strategies (Strength + Threat):**
How can we use strengths to defend against threats?

Example: "Use our data moat (strength) to defend against new entrants (threat)"

**WT Strategies (Weakness + Threat):**
How can we minimize weaknesses while avoiding threats?

Example: "Reduce burn rate (address weakness) to survive potential funding winter (threat)"

### Quality Guidelines

- Be specific and evidence-based, avoid generic statements
- Quantify where possible (market sizes, metrics, timelines)
- Note confidence levels: High (verified), Medium (reasonable basis), Low (assumption)
- Highlight contradictions or uncertainty
- Distinguish between current state and trajectory
- Consider multiple time horizons (short/medium/long-term)
- Balance honest assessment with actionable optimism
